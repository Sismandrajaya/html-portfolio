The Best Movies According to Raditya

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

Rating: 9.3/10

A powerful story about hope and friendship set in a prison. The pacing is slow but deeply emotional, with one of the most memorable endings in film history.

The Dark Knight

Rating: 9.0/10

More than just a superhero movie. It explores deep moral conflicts between Batman and the Joker. Heath Ledger’s performance as the Joker is iconic and unforgettable.

Parasite

Rating:8.6/10

A smart and layered film about social inequality. It blends genres—comedy, thriller, and drama—in a very unpredictable and well-executed way.
